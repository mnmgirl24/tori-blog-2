an introduction to me!

i’m tori, and i’ve been writing stories and ramblings since i was 5 years old. i’ve always loved coming up with elaborate tales based on my epic woes and whatever media i’m consuming at that point in time. some of my earliest works included twilight fanfiction posted on fanfiction.net, a star wars/runescape inspired story series, hades & persephone drabbles on ao3 (you can find me under hanleiasolo, if you feel so inclined), and various attempts at blogs and journals. most recently, i’ve been working on a macbeth adaptation set in 1960s kennedy era.
i’m also a children’s librarian (by accident) and an adjunct professor at two universities to teach for their first year writing programs. i have…never relaxed before in my life, ever.
while this website started as a specific blog, this is simply just my portfolio. please feel free to find me in other places. most recently, i've been on substack; you should answer the call over there! 

much love, tori
